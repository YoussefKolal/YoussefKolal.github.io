What is Extrusion ?
-
By Youssef Kolal

Extrusion is a process used to create objects of a fixed length, diameter and thickness by pushing raw material through a die which called an extruder, the idea is very simple
inside the extruder there's a screw that push melted raw material forward into a mold of desired shape like shown in the picture below :

The temperature of metling defers from material to other for exemple the Aluminium needs from 350 to 450 degree cilsius to melt while nickel needs a temperature from 1000 to 2000°C.
To enter more in details let's explain how the extruder works exaclty :
First of all the raw material enters form the feeder or the hopper which pass through a canal that leads to the screw that rotate to push the material forward, using heaters the raw material melts tto be able to pass through the die or the extruder head.

Let's take an exemple of the fabrication of the thermoplastics that are used in motor vehicule industry.
