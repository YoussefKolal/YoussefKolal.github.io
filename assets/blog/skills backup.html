Production Management

        Managing the production process involves taking into account many variables such as the workforce, equipment, raw materials, workflow, and more. Production management aims to optimize production and find a balancing point between the quality and capacity of produced goods, as well as the time and resources spent on producing them.
As production management requires the coordination and supervision of both people, materials, and equipment, managers need to continually make decisions in four key areas:
        - Production Planning.
        - Production Control.
        - Process Improvement.
        - Equipment Maintenance.
